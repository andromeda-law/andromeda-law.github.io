About Andromeda Law

Modern Legal Services for Today’s Clients

Andromeda Law was founded with a simple premise: legal services should adapt to how people actually live and work today. As an online-only law firm serving clients across Ontario, we’ve eliminated the inefficiencies of traditional law practice while maintaining the highest standards of professional service.

Our Approach

We believe that:

  • Your Time Matters: Legal services shouldn’t require taking time off work for in-person meetings when a video call would serve the same purpose.
  • Transparency is Essential: From our pricing to our processes, we believe in being clear and direct about what to expect.
  • Technology Should Serve You: We leverage modern tools not because they’re new, but because they make your experience better and more efficient.
  • Quality Doesn’t Require Overhead: By operating virtually, we eliminate unnecessary costs while maintaining professional excellence.
