Booking an Appointment

Scheduling a consultation with Andromeda Law is simple and convenient. Our online booking system allows you to choose a time that fits your schedule, ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience.

How to Book

  1. Choose Your Service: Select the type of legal consultation you need from our services page. This helps us prepare for your specific requirements.

  2. Pick a Date and Time: Use our online booking tool to view available time slots and choose one that works for you. We offer extended hours to accommodate various schedules.

  3. Complete the Booking Form: Provide your basic contact information and a brief description of your legal matter. This helps us understand your needs before the consultation.

  4. Receive a Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation with details about your appointment, including the Zoom link for your virtual meeting.

What to Expect During the Consultation

  • Virtual Meeting: Connect with us via Zoom or your preferred video conferencing tool. We will discuss your legal needs, answer any questions, and outline potential next steps.

  • Document Review: If applicable, we may ask you to upload relevant documents securely through our digital platform before the meeting. This helps us provide tailored advice during your consultation.

Post-Consultation Support

  • Follow-Up Communication: Stay connected through secure messaging platforms like Signal for quick and confidential updates. We are committed to providing ongoing support as your case progresses.

  • Next Steps: If you decide to retain our services, we will provide a clear plan of action, including timelines, cost estimates, and expected outcomes.

Booking Policies

Rescheduling: You can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through our booking tool or by contacting us directly.

Cancellation: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment may be subject to a fee. Please review our cancellation policy for more details.
