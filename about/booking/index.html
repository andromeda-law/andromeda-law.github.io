Booking an Appointment

Scheduling a consultation with Andromeda Law is straightforward and convenient. All bookings are handled through our online platform on LawTap, allowing you to choose the service you need and a time that fits your schedule.

If you need or want an appointment outside the listed times on LawTap, or for assistance or questions about booking, feel free to email us at info@andromeda.law, or text us at 548-776-0070 by SMS or Signal. See our contact page for full contact information.

How to Book

  1. Visit Our LawTap Page: Go to our online booking tool to begin the booking process.

  2. Choose Your Service: Select the type of legal service you require. Options include:

    • Employment Contract Review
    • Contract Drafting
    • 15-Minute Consultation (for matters billed at our hourly rate)
    • Appointments for Clients on Retainer

  3. Select a Date and Time: Pick an available time slot that suits your schedule. We offer extended hours, including evenings and weekends.

  4. Provide Your Information: Fill out the booking form with your contact details and any relevant information about your legal matter.

  5. Complete Payment (If Applicable): For services requiring upfront payment (such as flat-fee services), you will be directed to a secure Stripe payment page after booking. If you wish to pay by a method other than Stripe (such as Interac E-Transfer or Cryptocurrency), please contact us at info@andromeda.law after booking.

  6. Receive Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation with all the details of your appointment, including a Zoom link if applicable.

Service Options

15-Minute Consultation (Free)

For matters billed at our hourly rate, we offer a free 15-minute consultation. This session is intended to determine whether our services are a good fit for your needs. Please note that legal advice will not be provided during this initial consultation; it is an opportunity to understand your situation, discuss potential next steps and discuss costs.

Flat-Fee Services

For services like employment contract reviews or drafting, select the appropriate option when booking. Payment is required upfront to confirm your appointment. These services include:

  • Employment Contract Review
  • Employment Contract Drafting

Clients on Retainer

If you are an existing client with a retainer agreement, you can book appointments or phone calls directly through LawTap by selecting the relevant option.

Meeting Formats

  • Virtual Meetings (Zoom): For most appointments, LawTap will generate a Zoom link for a virtual meeting. You’ll find this link in your confirmation email.

  • Phone Calls: If you book a phone consultation, we will call you at the scheduled time using the phone number you provide during booking.

After Booking

  • Email/SMS Confirmation: You’ll receive a confirmation email with all appointment details and any necessary instructions.

  • Document Submission: For certain services, you may be asked to upload relevant documents prior to your appointment. Instructions on how to do this will be provided.

  • Appointment Reminders: Automated reminders will be sent to by email and SMS help you remember your scheduled time.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the booking process or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We’re here to help you navigate the process smoothly.

Ready to Book?

Visit our online booking tool to view available time slots and choose the service that works best for you.

Note: All appointments are subject to our Cancellation Policy. Please review it to understand our policies regarding rescheduling and cancellations.
