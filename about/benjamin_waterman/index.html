About Benjamin Waterman

Dedicated to Providing Clear and Effective Legal Representation

Benjamin Miller Waterman has been practicing law in Ontario since 2018, bringing over six years of experience across a range of legal matters. He has represented a diverse range of clients, from individuals to large corporations. His practice focuses on civil litigation and employment law, providing strategic and practical advice to help clients navigate complex legal challenges.

Professional Background

Benjamin graduated from Western Law at the University of Western Ontario in 2017 and was called to the Bar of Ontario in 2018. Before establishing Andromeda Law, he practiced at a full-service law firm, handling a diverse caseload. His experience includes:

  • Representing clients in various civil disputes, from contract issues to complex business litigation.
  • Advising both employees and employers on employment law matters, such as wrongful dismissal, workplace policies, and human rights issues.
  • Handling cases at all levels of the Ontario civil court system, including Small Claims Court, the Superior Court of Justice, and the Ontario Court of Appeal

Approach to Law

Benjamin’s approach is rooted in accessibility, transparency, and client-focused service. He leverages digital tools to streamline the legal process, making it easier for clients to stay informed and engaged in their cases. His commitment to clear communication and practical advice ensures that clients understand their options and can make informed decisions.

  • Transparent Communication: Clients are kept in the loop with straightforward updates and clear explanations of their legal matters.
  • Efficient Processes: Utilizing digital tools for document management and secure communication, reducing the need for time-consuming in-person meetings.
  • Client-Centered Service: By focusing on each client’s unique needs, Benjamin provides tailored legal strategies that align with their goals.
