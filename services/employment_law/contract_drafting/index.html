Employment Contract Drafting

Clear and comprehensive employment contracts are essential for setting expectations and protecting your business. Andromeda Law assists employers in drafting tailored employment agreements that comply with Ontario laws.

Our Contract Drafting Services

  • Custom Contracts: Creating agreements tailored to your business needs and industry requirements.
  • Legal Compliance: Ensuring contracts meet all legal standards, including recent changes in employment law.
  • Key Provisions: Including clauses on confidentiality, non-compete, termination, and more to protect your interests.
  • Consultation and Revisions: Working with you to understand your needs and incorporating feedback into the final document.

Flat-Fee Pricing

  • Base Fee: $1,000 for drafting a new employment contract.
  • Additional Employees: $250 for each additional employee contract.

Our Approach

  • Collaborative Process: Engaging with you to ensure the contract aligns with your business goals.
  • Clarity and Precision: Writing in clear language to minimize misunderstandings.
  • Efficiency: Delivering high-quality contracts within agreed timelines.
