General Employment Law Advice

Employment relationships can present various legal challenges. Andromeda Law offers general legal advice to both employees and employers on a wide range of employment law matters.

For Employees

  • Wrongful Dismissal: Advising on your rights if you’ve been terminated without just cause or adequate notice.
  • Workplace Harassment and Discrimination: Guidance on addressing and resolving issues related to harassment or discrimination.
  • Leaves of Absence: Understanding your rights regarding medical leave, parental leave, and other statutory leaves.

For Employers

  • Workplace Policies: Assisting in developing compliant policies on harassment, discrimination, and workplace safety.
  • Termination Procedures: Advising on lawful termination practices to minimize legal risks.
  • Employment Standards Compliance: Ensuring your business adheres to the Employment Standards Act and other relevant legislation.

Our Approach

  • Personalized Consultation: Offering advice tailored to your specific situation and needs.
  • Proactive Strategies: Helping you prevent issues before they arise through informed planning.
  • Confidential Support: Maintaining strict confidentiality in all matters.

Billing

  • Hourly Rate: Services are billed at our general hourly rate of $300 per hour.
