Employment Contract Review

Before signing an employment contract, it’s crucial to understand the terms and implications fully. Andromeda Law offers thorough contract review services to help you make informed decisions about your employment.

Our Contract Review Services

  • Comprehensive Analysis: Reviewing all clauses, including compensation, benefits, termination provisions, and non-compete agreements.
  • Identifying Risks: Highlighting any unfavorable terms or potential legal issues that may affect your rights.
  • Negotiation Advice: Providing guidance on how to negotiate better terms or request amendments.
  • Plain Language Explanation: Translating legal jargon into clear language so you understand exactly what you’re agreeing to.

Flat-Fee Pricing

  • Service Fee: $350 (exclusive of HST)

Our Approach

  • Timely Service: We understand that job offers may have tight deadlines and strive to provide prompt reviews.
  • Personalized Advice: Tailoring our recommendations to your specific situation and career goals.
  • Confidentiality: Maintaining strict confidentiality throughout the process.
