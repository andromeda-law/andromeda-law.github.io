Estates Litigation

Dealing with disputes over wills and estates can be emotionally taxing and legally complex. At Andromeda Law, we provide compassionate and strategic representation to help you navigate these sensitive matters.

Our Services in Estates Litigation

  • Will Contests: Challenging or defending the validity of a will based on factors like undue influence, lack of testamentary capacity, or improper execution.
  • Dependant Support Claims: Assisting dependants who have been inadequately provided for in a will to secure fair support from the estate.
  • Executor and Trustee Disputes: Resolving conflicts involving executors, trustees, and beneficiaries over the management and distribution of estate assets.
  • Estate Administration Issues: Addressing concerns related to the administration of the estate, including asset valuation and debt settlement.

Our Approach

  • Compassionate Guidance: We understand the emotional complexities involved and provide support with sensitivity.
  • Strategic Planning: Developing tailored legal strategies to achieve your goals efficiently.
  • Clear Communication: Keeping you informed at every stage to ensure you understand your options.
