Legal Services Tailored to Your Needs

At Andromeda Law, we provide a range of legal services designed to help you navigate complex legal issues efficiently and effectively. Whether you’re dealing with a dispute, need advice on employment matters, or require assistance with contract-related issues, we’re here to support you. Our online-only practice allows us to offer these services virtually, ensuring convenience and flexibility for clients across Ontario.

Practice Areas

Estates Litigation

Disputes over wills and estates can be emotionally and financially draining. We offer compassionate and strategic support in handling:

  • Will Contests: Challenging or defending the validity of a will based on undue influence, lack of capacity, or improper execution.
  • Beneficiary Disputes: Resolving conflicts over the interpretation of a will or distribution of estate assets.
  • Executor Issues: Assisting executors or beneficiaries with disputes over the management of the estate.

Our goal is to resolve these matters efficiently, helping families navigate these challenging situations with as little stress as possible.

Employment Law

Whether you’re an employee or an employer, understanding your rights and obligations is crucial. We provide comprehensive employment law services, including:

  • Employee Representation: If you’ve been wrongfully dismissed, harassed, or faced discrimination, we can help you seek fair compensation and a resolution.
  • Employment Contract Review: For employees, we review job offers and contracts to ensure your rights are protected before you sign.
  • Employer Services: We draft clear, legally sound employment contracts and provide advice on workplace policies, compliance, and conflict resolution.

From new hires to complex workplace disputes, we’re here to help you navigate the legal landscape of employment.

Contract Disputes

Contract issues can disrupt your business or personal life. We assist with:

  • Breach of Contract: Representing clients in disputes where one party has failed to fulfill their contractual obligations.
  • Negotiation and Settlement: Helping resolve contract disputes through negotiation to avoid costly litigation when possible.
  • Contract Review and Drafting: Ensuring that your contracts are clear, enforceable, and aligned with your goals, whether for business agreements, service contracts, or other arrangements.

We aim to protect your interests and achieve fair outcomes, whether you’re enforcing a contract or defending against a claim.

Collections

Unpaid debts can strain your business. Our collections services are designed to recover what you’re owed quickly and legally:

  • Demand Letters and Negotiation: Sending formal demands and negotiating with debtors to settle outstanding balances.
  • Small Claims Court: Representing clients in Small Claims Court for debts under $35,000, ensuring a cost-effective and efficient recovery process.
  • Legal Action: Taking appropriate legal steps, including garnishments or property liens, to enforce payment when necessary.

Our focus is on recovering your funds with minimal disruption to your business operations.

Small Claims

Small Claims Court is a venue for resolving disputes under $35,000, including unpaid invoices, contract issues, and property damage. We offer:

  • Comprehensive Representation: Handling all aspects of your Small Claims case, from filing to court appearances.
  • Document Preparation: Drafting clear and persuasive claims, defenses, and evidence submissions.
  • Settlement Negotiation: Attempting to resolve disputes through negotiation and mediation to avoid court when possible.

We make the Small Claims process straightforward and manageable, allowing you to focus on what matters most.
