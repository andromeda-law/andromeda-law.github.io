Practice Areas

At Andromeda Law, we specialize in providing comprehensive legal services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our practice areas encompass a range of legal disciplines, ensuring that you receive expert guidance and representation in the following fields:

Litigation

We offer strategic and effective litigation services to help you navigate legal disputes with confidence.

  • Estates Litigation: Handling disputes over wills, estates, and trusts with sensitivity and expertise.
  • Superior Court: Representing clients in complex civil litigation cases at the Superior Court of Justice.
  • Small Claims: Providing efficient representation in Small Claims Court for disputes under $35,000.
  • Collections: Assisting businesses and individuals in recovering owed debts promptly and legally.

Employment Law

Navigating the complexities of employment relationships requires specialized knowledge. We assist both employees and employers with:

  • Contract Review: Reviewing employment contracts to ensure your rights and interests are protected.
  • Contract Drafting: Crafting clear and enforceable employment agreements tailored to your needs.
  • General Legal Advice: Providing guidance on a wide range of employment law matters, including workplace policies and dispute resolution.
